Chicken Slayer Z

About the Game

A quirky player versus computer game with a chicken invaders theme.

This is a story based game consisting of multiple stages:

    Gameplay

    Fight your way towards freeing the chickens on earth from the wrath of the chicken slayers!

    Defeat 3 bosses each successivly stronger and faster than the previous in order to achieve victory!

    Story

    Experience an... interesting storyline...

    A series of visuals to add to the already peculiar story and hopefully give the player a laugh.

    A world message

    In aim to somehow inspire you to become a better global citizen and unite everyone in the event of a real life Chicken Slayer attack!

    A Website

    A website which was created using a premade layout in aim to advertise this game, give a summary of the developers and a little more about the storyline.
